Group: Sonata




A sonata written for a solo piano. Piano sonatas are usually written in three or four movements, although some piano sonatas have been written with a single movement (Scarlatti, Scriabin), two movements (Mozart, Haydn), five (Brahms' Third Piano Sonata) or even more movements. The first movement is generally composed in sonata form.
