Canzone

~1230
Italy, France



Group: Song
Origin: Madrigal
Influenced: Canzona

Synonyms: Канцона, cancione




An Italian or Provensal song or ballad. It is also used to describe a type of lyric which resembles a madrigal. Sometimes a composition which is simple and songlike is designated as a canzone, especially if it is by a non-Italian. Close to troubadour song.
