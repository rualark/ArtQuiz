Villanella

~1540 (Renaissance)
Italy



Group: Song
Influenced: Monody, Canzonetta




A form of light Italian secular vocal music which originated in Italy just before the middle of the 16th century. It first appeared in Naples, and influenced the later canzonetta, and from there also influenced the madrigal.
