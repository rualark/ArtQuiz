Commedia dell'arte

1551
Italy



Group: Play
Influenced: Opera buffa

Synonyms: Комедия дель арте




"A form of theater characterized by masked ""types"" which began in Italy in the 16th century and was responsible for the advent of the actress and improvised performances based on sketches or scenarios. Was performed by professional actors (comici) who perfected a specific role or mask. Conversely, commedia erudita was performed by amateurs. Improvisatory."
